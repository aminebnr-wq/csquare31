About CSQUARE³

Where Innovation Meets Creativity

Our Mission

CSQUARE³ is Annaba's premier technology and creativity club, bringing together passionate students who code, create, and compile innovative solutions. We are a community of developers, designers, filmmakers, and digital artists pushing the boundaries of what's possible.


Based in Annaba, Algeria – Made by students, for students. We believe in the power of collaboration, continuous learning, and creative expression through technology.


What We Do

• Coding: Web & App Development, AI/ML, Competitive Programming
• Design: UI/UX, Graphic Design, Branding
• Film-making: Video Production, Editing, Storytelling
• Innovation: Hackathons, Workshops, Tech Events
