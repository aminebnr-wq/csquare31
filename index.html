About CSQUARE³ Agency

Digital services & creative solutions

Our Mission

CSQUARE³ Agency provides high-quality digital services: programming, graphic design, video editing, and photography. We connect clients with talented students and freelancers who deliver professional results.


Based in Annaba, Algeria — We focus on practical outcomes: product-ready websites, strong visual brands, engaging video content, and professional photos.


What We Offer

• Programming: Websites, mobile apps, automation, integrations.
• Graphic Design: Logos, branding, posters, social visuals.
• Video: Editing, promos, short-form content.
• Photography: Product, event, portrait shoots.
